void bl::entity::IdGenerator::generateSequentialIds ( bool  makeSequential)
static

Sets whether new Entities should be created with random or sequential ids. Sequential ids are guaranteed to be unique, but require a mutex and may slow down threads that have to wait for a lock. Random ids have a 1/2^64 chance of collision and are the default setting.

Parameters
makeSequentialTrue to generate ids sequentially, false for random
