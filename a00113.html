class  bl::event::Dispatcher
 Event dispatcher capable of handling events of many different types. The dispatcher is threadsafe. If events are dispatched by multiple threads care should be taken to keep the listeners threadsafe as well. Care must also be taken to not subscribe the same listener more than once or it will get notified more than once per event. More...
