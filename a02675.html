template<typename TKey , typename TPayload >
enum bl::ctr::Cache::EvictionPolicy

Policy that dictates which items are removed when the high watermark is reached.

Enumerator
LeastRecentlyAccessed 
LeastRecentlyModified 

Items that were not read or written recently are purged.

FirstInFirstOut 

Items that were not written recently are purged.

Items are purged in the same order they were added in
